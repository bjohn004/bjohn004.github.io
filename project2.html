World Mental Health and Wealth Summary

Using publically available data on GDP and suicide rates, my project team put together an analysis to with the hyopthesis that Countries with lower GDP would result in higher suicide rates. The analysis used linear regressions to determine if there were correlations. Based on our analysis the null hypotheses was rejected such that there is statistical significance between the two categories. However, the significance was not strong based on our r-squared values which makes sense that there may be many other factors beyond economic factors of a country that determines the mental health and suicide rate impacts that a country might have.
